Banana Loaf

    Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 2 eggs beaten
  • 3 large bananas mashed
  • 1 1/2 cup flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla

    • Optional

  • 1/4 cup chocolate chips
  • 1/4 cup walnut pieces
  • 1/2 tsp cinnamon

    Method

  1. Pre-heat the over to 350F
  2. Cream together butter and sugar
  3. Add eggs and mashed banana
  4. Sift together flour, baking soda and salt
  5. Combine the wet and dry ingredients and add any extras
  6. Pour into a greased loaf pan and bake for 60 minuites
