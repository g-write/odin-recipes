Classic Brownies

    Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cup sugar
  • 3/4 cup white sugar
  • 3/4 cup flour
  • 2/3 cup cocoa powder
  • 1/2 cup powdered sugar
  • 1/2 cup dark chocolate chips
  • 3/4 tsp salt
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup canola oil
  • 2 tbs water
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla

    Method

  1. Pre-heat the over to 325F
  2. Line an 8x8 inch baking dish with parchment paper
  3. Combine sugar, flour, cocoa powder, powdered sugar, chocolate chips and salt
  4. Whisk together eggs, oil, water and vanilla
  5. Combine the wet and dry mixes
  6. Pour batter into the baking dish
  7. Bake for 40 - 48 min or until a toothpivk comes out with only a few crumbs

    8. Tip: It is better to brownies out early than over-cook
