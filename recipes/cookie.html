Chocolate Oatmeal Cookies

    Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 3/4 cup white sugar
  • 3/4 brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 1/2 cup flour
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/4 tsp baking soda
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 2 cup oatmeal
  • Dash of vanilla
  • Dark chocloat broken into pieces

    Method

  1. Pre-heat the over to 350F
  2. Cream together butter and sugar
  3. Whisk eggs and vanilla add to mixture
  4. Sift flour, baking powder, baking soda and salt
  5. Combine the wet and dry ingredients and add the oatmeal
  6. Add the broken chocolate pieces
  7. Mould into 1 inch balls and arrange on a baking sheet
  8. Bake in batches for 8-10 minuites

    9. Tip: Remove from the oven before they looks fully done for extra soft cookies. They will continue to cook once out of the oven
